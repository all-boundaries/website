## Context

In a distributed system architecture, a service typically can't provide all the
value by itself. This leads to integrations with other members of the
ecosystem increasing the communication needs and the system complexity.

The bigger the system, the more integrations will exist. A team will never going
to be able to know the details of the whole system however the team should be an
expert on how their service works and in what it provides.

Making the team and service boundary explicit is critical for success. It
enables everyone to see what each one is responsible for and where the lines are
drawn leading to frutful discussions on how to guarantee the integration works
as expected.
